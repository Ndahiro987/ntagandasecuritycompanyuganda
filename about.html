Our Story

Welcome to Ntaganda Security Group, a dedicated team committed to providing top-tier security solutions across Uganda. Founded on the principles of trust, integrity, and unwavering protection, we understand the unique security landscape of our nation and strive to offer services that are both effective and tailored to your specific needs.

Our journey began with a vision to create a safer environment for businesses, individuals, and communities. With years of combined experience in law enforcement, security management, and risk assessment, our leadership team has built a company that prioritizes professionalism and client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional security services that exceed expectations, ensuring the safety and peace of mind of our clients through proactive measures, highly trained personnel, and advanced technology.

Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted security provider in Uganda, recognized for our reliability, innovation, and commitment to safeguarding our nation.

At Ntaganda Security Group, we believe in building strong, lasting relationships with our clients. We take the time to understand your unique security concerns and develop customized strategies to address them effectively. Our commitment extends beyond just providing security; we aim to be a partner you can rely on.
