Join Our Team

At Ntaganda Security Group Uganda, we are always looking for talented and dedicated individuals to join our team. If you are passionate about security and committed to excellence, we encourage you to explore the opportunities below.

Security Guard

We are seeking reliable and vigilant security guards to protect our clients' premises. Responsibilities include patrolling, monitoring surveillance equipment, and ensuring a safe environment.

Requirements: High school diploma, clean criminal record, good physical condition, excellent communication skills.

Site Supervisor

We are looking for experienced site supervisors to oversee security operations at various client locations. Responsibilities include managing security teams, ensuring adherence to protocols, and client communication.

Requirements: Proven experience in a supervisory role, strong leadership skills, excellent organizational abilities, security certifications preferred.

Apply for Site Supervisor

Mobile Patrol Officer

Join our mobile patrol team to conduct regular checks of client properties. You will be responsible for deterring threats and responding to incidents as needed.

Requirements: Valid driver's license, security experience preferred, strong observational skills, ability to work independently.

Apply for Mobile Patrol Officer

Surveillance System Technician

We need skilled technicians to install, maintain, and repair our advanced surveillance systems. Knowledge of CCTV and access control systems is essential.

Requirements: Technical diploma or relevant certification, experience with security systems, problem-solving skills.
